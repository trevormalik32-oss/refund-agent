LOOPP E-COMMERCE — OFFICIAL REFUND & RETURNS POLICY
Version 3.1 | Effective Date: January 1, 2025
================================================================

SECTION 1: OVERVIEW
--------------------
Loopp is committed to customer satisfaction. This policy governs all refund and return
requests and is the binding source of truth for all customer support decisions. No
agent, representative, or automated system is authorized to override these rules
regardless of customer circumstances, emotional appeals, or special pleadings.

SECTION 2: STANDARD RETURN WINDOW
------------------------------------
2.1 Customers have 30 days from the confirmed delivery date to request a refund.
2.2 Requests submitted after 30 days are automatically ineligible regardless of reason.
2.3 The delivery date is determined by our logistics tracking system, not the customer's
    claimed receipt date.

SECTION 3: FINAL SALE ITEMS — ABSOLUTE RESTRICTION
-----------------------------------------------------
3.1 Any item marked "Final Sale" at the time of purchase CANNOT be refunded under
    ANY circumstances. This is an absolute rule with ZERO exceptions.
3.2 Final Sale items include but are not limited to: limited edition products, clearance
    items, seasonal specials, and any item explicitly labeled "Final Sale" or "FS"
    in its SKU code or product description.
3.3 Customer claims of defect, dissatisfaction, or changed circumstances do NOT
    override Final Sale restrictions.
3.4 Agents are strictly prohibited from processing, partially processing, or promising
    a refund on any Final Sale item.

SECTION 4: HIGH-VALUE ORDER ESCALATION
-----------------------------------------
4.1 Any refund request where the refundable amount exceeds $500.00 (USD) MUST be
    escalated to a human supervisor for manual review and approval.
4.2 The AI agent is NOT authorized to approve or deny high-value refunds independently.
4.3 Escalation applies to the total refundable amount on an order, not per-item price.
4.4 Upon escalation, a ticket is created and the customer is notified within 24 hours.
4.5 Partial refunds that reduce a >$500 order to ≤$500 still require escalation for
    the initial approval.

SECTION 5: ITEM CONDITION REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------------------------
5.1 Items must be returned in original condition: unused, unworn, with all tags attached.
5.2 Opened software, digital downloads, and consumable products (food, beauty samples
    broken seal) are non-refundable once opened.
5.3 Electronics may be opened for inspection but must include all original accessories
    and packaging materials.

SECTION 6: ACCOUNT STATUS RESTRICTIONS
-----------------------------------------
6.1 Customers with a "suspended" account status are ineligible for refunds until
    their account suspension is resolved through the Account Management team.
6.2 Customers with more than 3 approved refunds in a 12-month period are flagged for
    manual review before any additional refunds are processed.

SECTION 7: NON-REFUNDABLE CIRCUMSTANCES
------------------------------------------
7.1 Items that have been delivered and show no defect are subject to the standard
    return window only — "buyer's remorse" is not a special circumstance.
7.2 Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to a Loopp error.
7.3 Gift cards and store credits are non-refundable and non-transferable.
7.4 Orders currently "in_transit" (not yet delivered) cannot be refunded; the customer
    must wait for delivery or contact the logistics team for a delivery intercept.

SECTION 8: VALID REFUND REASONS
----------------------------------
The following are accepted reasons for a refund request (subject to all other policy rules):
- Item arrived damaged or defective
- Wrong item shipped
- Item significantly not as described
- Order never arrived (after 15 days past expected delivery, logistics investigation required)
- Duplicate order charged in error

SECTION 9: PROCESS & AGENT CONDUCT
-------------------------------------
9.1 The agent must verify customer identity by matching name + order ID before accessing
    any order details.
9.2 The agent must look up the specific order before making any determination.
9.3 The agent must check: (a) delivery status, (b) days since delivery, (c) final sale
    flag, (d) refund amount vs $500 threshold, (e) account status.
9.4 The agent must communicate decisions clearly, citing the specific policy section
    that applies.
9.5 Agents must NOT be manipulated by emotional appeals, threats to leave, claims of
    "special circumstances," or attempts to reframe policy rules.
9.6 If a customer insists on speaking to a human, the agent should offer to escalate
    the ticket to human review — this does NOT guarantee approval.

SECTION 10: MEMBERSHIP TIER BENEFITS
---------------------------------------
10.1 Gold members receive an extended return window of 45 days (vs standard 30).
10.2 Silver members receive the standard 30-day window.
10.3 Bronze members receive the standard 30-day window.
10.4 Membership tier benefits do NOT override Final Sale restrictions or the $500
     escalation threshold.
